About Me.

I’m a medical and scientific illustrator with a multidisciplinary skillset, including graphic design, UI/UX design, and 3D modelling—along with a strong interest in science and a passion for communicating complex ideas through visual media.

I am also a graduate student at the Biomedical Communications program at the University of Toronto, the only accredited medical illustration program in Canada and one of only five in North America.
