DermAlert: Skincare Product Repository

A personalized skincare product repository for people with skin sensitivities
Role: Solo Researcher & Designer Tools: Figma, Miro, Excel Timeline: Jan â€“ Mar 2025
Overview

DermAlert is a web-based repository that helps sufferers of skin sensitivities easily identify, filter out, and avoid their allergens. Users can upload patch test results, filter products by ingredient, and contribute to a growing community database.

Problem

Individuals with skin sensitivities often struggle to avoid allergens in everyday skincare products. Patch test results are hard to translate to ingredient lists, and existing platforms are incomplete or require tedious manual input.

Solution

DermAlert provides tailored search results based on user sensitivities, recognizes ingredient synonyms, and allows users to contribute new products, making it easier to find safe skincare options.

Impact

DermAlert empowers users to make informed skincare choices, reduces allergic reactions, and provides a resource dermatologists can recommend to patients.
