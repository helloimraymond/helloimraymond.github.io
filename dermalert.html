DermAlert: Skincare Product Repository

Audience: Individuals suffering from allergy-based skin sensitivities

Tools: Figma, Miro, Excel

Role: Solo researcher & designer

DermAlert is a personalized web-based repository that helps sufferers of skin sensitivities easily identify, filter out, and avoid their allergens.



Problem.
 Individuals with skin sensitivities (eczema, rosacea, etc.) often face challenges in managing their condition after undergoing patch tests, as they receive minimal support to help them effectively avoid allergens/irritants in everyday skincare products.

Solution.
 DermAlert is a personalized web-based repository that helps sufferers of skin sensitivities easily identify and avoid allergens in skincare product ingredients by offering tailored search results based on their unique sensitivities and patch test findings.

Impact.
 With this personalized tool, I hope to empower individuals to make informed skincare choices, reducing reactions and improving skin health. I also wish to receive input from experts to make DermAlert a resource dermatologists feel comfortable recommending to patients.
